General The Board of Directors believes that it is in the best interest of the Company and the shareholders, and is hereby soliciting shareholder approval, to approve giving the Board of Directors discretion to effect a share consolidation of the Company’s authorized and issued shares, at a ratio of 15 -for-1 (the “ Share Consolidation ”), on such date as the Board of Directors shall determine and no later than the next annual general meeting of the Company (the “ Effective Date ”). The Share Consolidation must be approved by ordinary resolution which requires the affirmative vote of a simple majority of the votes cast by such shareholders as, being entitled to do so, vote in person or, where proxies are allowed, by proxy or, in the case of corporations, by their duly authorized representative, at the Meeting. If the Company’s shareholders approve this proposal, the Board of Directors will have authority to implement the Share Consolidation at any time after the approval of the Share Consolidation and before the Company’s next annual general meeting, at a ratio of 15 -for-1 . The Share Consolidation will be implemented simultaneously for all authorized, issued and outstanding shares.
